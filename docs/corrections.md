# Corrections

Tombstone Research publishes a log of every correction made to Fund Autopsy's data, analysis, or published research after initial release. Each entry lists the date, the prior value, the correction, and the source filing or methodology change that drove the update.

A correction is logged whenever a previously-published number, conflict flag, or narrative claim is found to be wrong or materially misleading. Methodology updates that change estimated values on a fund's page are logged here when the change exceeds a material threshold.

## How to report an error

Spot something that looks wrong? Open an issue at [github.com/tombstoneresearch/fund-autopsy/issues](https://github.com/tombstoneresearch/fund-autopsy/issues) with the ticker, the field, and the filing reference. We review every report and post the disposition here.
